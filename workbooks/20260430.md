### 英文パッセージ

My name is Anna. I am a student. Every morning, I wake up and check my phone. My phone is very important for my day. Many people like me use smartphones every day. They help us in many ways.

Smartphones are useful tools. We can talk to our friends and family. We can send short messages to them. We can also read the news from many places in the world. We can watch funny videos too. The internet is a big place with much information. We can learn new things quickly. We can find new places to visit for our next holiday. Sometimes, we play games on our phones. These games are fun for a short time.

But it is important to take a break from our phones. We should not use them too much. It is good to talk to people face to face. We need to go outside and see nature. We can play sports with friends or read a paper book. These things are also important.

Technology, like smartphones and the internet, is very good. It helps us in many ways, for school and for work. But our life is more than just technology. We must find a good balance between them. This balance helps us to live a happy and healthy life. So, use your phone wisely, but also remember to enjoy the real world around you.
