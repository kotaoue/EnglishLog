## 本日の問題

## テーマ: エミリーの日々の生活

### 英文パッセージ

My name is Emily. I am a student. I live in a big city. My home is not far from my school. I go to school every day.
I like to study English. It is a little difficult, but it is fun. My teacher is kind. She helps us a lot.
After school, I often go to the park with my friend, Sarah. We like to walk there. We talk about many things. Sarah is a good friend.
On the weekend, I have more free time. On Saturday morning, I usually help my mother. We clean the house together. In the afternoon, I sometimes read books. I like adventure stories.
On Sunday, I often visit my grandmother. She lives in a small house near the station. I like to talk with her. She tells me old stories. She is very nice.
I also like to cook. My mother teaches me. Today, we want to make a cake. It is a chocolate cake. I like sweet food.
My life is busy, but it is happy. I learn new things every day. I meet nice people. I think it is important to enjoy every day.

### 設問

1. エミリーは週末に、お母さんと一緒に何をしますか？
   - あなたの回答: 家の掃除

2. エミリーは英語の勉強について、どのように感じていますか？
   - あなたの回答: ちょっと難しいけど楽しい

3. エミリーは今日、お母さんと一緒に何を作る予定ですか？
   - あなたの回答: チョコレートケーキ

4. パッセージに出てくる "kind" という単語は、どのような意味合いで使われていますか？
   - あなたの回答: 先生が親切

5. このパッセージから、エミリーが自分の日々の生活について主に何を伝えたいと考えていると思いますか？
   - あなたの回答: 新しいこと学ぶのと、良い人に会うのが重要
