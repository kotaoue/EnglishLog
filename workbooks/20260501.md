## 本日の問題

先生、かしこまりました。中学1〜2年レベルの基本単語のみを使用し、1〜2文の短い英文で構成された英語問題を作成します。

## テーマ: 私の楽しい週末 (My Fun Weekends)

### 英文パッセージ

My name is Ken. I am a student. I like weekends very much.
On Saturday morning, I wake up late. I eat a big breakfast with my family. My mom makes good pancakes. I like them.
After breakfast, I often read a book. I like books about animals. My dog, Pochi, sleeps near me. He is cute.
In the afternoon, I go to the park with my friends. We play soccer there. We also talk and laugh a lot. It is fun. Sometimes, we eat ice cream.
In the evening, I watch a movie at home. My father and mother watch it with me. We eat popcorn.

On Sunday, I clean my room. It is important to keep my room clean. Then, I study English for an hour. English is a little difficult, but I like it. I want to read English books more.
In the afternoon, I sometimes go to the library. I borrow new books there. Or I visit my grandmother's house. She makes tasty cookies.
In the evening, I prepare for school on Monday. I check my bag and homework. I go to bed early.
My weekends are always busy but happy. I like spending time with my family and friends. Weekends are great!

### 設問

1. ケンは土曜日の午後に友達と何をしに行きますか？
    - あなたの回答: サッカーをしに公園に行く

2. ケンは日曜日の午後に何をすることがありますか？2つ答えてください。
    - あなたの回答: 図書館に行く。おばあちゃんの家に行く。

3. ケンは自分の週末についてどのように感じていますか？
    - あなたの回答: 忙しいけど楽しい。

4. 英文中の "busy" はどのような意味ですか？日本語で答えてください。
    - あなたの回答: 忙しい

5. この英文パッセージは、ケンの何について書かれていますか？
    - あなたの回答: 週末の過ごし方
