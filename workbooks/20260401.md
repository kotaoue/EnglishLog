## テーマ: 健康的な毎日 (A Healthy Daily Life)

### 英文パッセージ

Hello. My name is Alex. I want to live a healthy life every day. It is important for my body and my mind.

In the morning, I always wake up early. I like to see the sun. After that, I eat a good breakfast. I usually eat bread, eggs, and fruit. A healthy breakfast gives me a lot of energy for my school day.

I walk to school. It takes about 20 minutes. Walking is a simple exercise. It is good for my legs and my heart. When I walk, I listen to music or just enjoy the fresh air.

At school, I study many subjects. English, Math, and Science are my favorites. Studying is good for my brain. During break time, I often play outside with my friends. We run and jump. Moving our bodies is fun and keeps us strong.

In the afternoon, I always drink a lot of water. Our bodies need water to work well. I also try to eat healthy snacks, like apples or carrots. They are good for my health. I do not eat many sweets.

After school, I sometimes help my mom in the kitchen. We cook dinner together. Eating fresh vegetables and meat is good.

In the evening, I usually read a book or draw pictures. I try not to use my smartphone for a long time. It is important to rest my eyes.

I go to bed early, around nine o'clock. Sleep is very important for a healthy body. I need about eight hours of sleep every night.

Living a healthy life helps me feel happy and strong. I can do many things. This simple daily routine helps me every day.

### 設問

1. Alexは毎朝、朝食に何を食べることが多いですか？
   - あなたの回答: パンと卵

2. Alexはなぜ水をたくさん飲むことが大切だと言っていますか？
   - あなたの回答: 身体がいい感じに動くために必要だから

3. Alexは夜、寝る前にスマートフォンを長時間使わないようにしています。その理由は何ですか？
   - あなたの回答: 目を休めるため

4. パッセージ中の "simple exercise" とはどのような運動のことですか？具体的に本文から例を挙げて説明してください。
   - あなたの回答: ウォーキング

5. このパッセージでAlexが私たちに伝えたい最も大切なメッセージは何だと思いますか？
   - あなたの回答: 毎日できるちょっとした健康習慣
