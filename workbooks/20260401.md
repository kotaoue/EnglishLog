## テーマ: 良い一日を始めるための朝の習慣

### 英文パッセージ

My name is Ken. I like mornings very much. I think a good morning routine makes a good day. Every day, I wake up early. I want to start my day well.

First, I always drink a glass of water. This is good for my body. It helps me wake up. Then, I open the window. I breathe fresh air. This makes me feel healthy and active.

After that, I do some easy exercises. I stretch my arms and legs. This is not hard. It helps my body to move. I feel my muscles get ready.

Next, I make my breakfast. I usually eat fruit and yogurt. Sometimes I also eat a piece of toast. Breakfast gives me energy. It is very important for my work or study.

After breakfast, I read a small book. Or I listen to some English music. This helps my brain. It makes me calm. I learn new words every morning.

These morning habits are very important to me. They make my day happy and productive. I feel less stress. I can do many things well. I recommend you try to find your own good morning routine! A small start can change your day.
