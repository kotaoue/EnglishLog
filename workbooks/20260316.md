## テーマ: 新しい趣味を見つける

### 英文パッセージ

Emily is a high school student who often feels a bit bored on weekends. She has some free time after finishing her homework, and she wants to use it in a more exciting way. "I need a new hobby," she thought. "Something that makes me happy and helps me learn new things."

First, Emily considered **reading**. She loves stories and visiting new worlds through books. "Reading can broaden my horizons," she said to herself. "I can learn about history, science, or even just enjoy a good fantasy novel." She imagined spending quiet afternoons at the library or reading a thrilling e-book on her tablet.

Next, she thought about **cooking**. Her mom often made delicious meals, and Emily wanted to learn that skill. "It would be great to bake cakes or make dinner for my family," she mused. Cooking would be a creative activity, and she could try recipes from different cultures. Plus, everyone loves good food!

Finally, Emily also considered **hiking**. She loves nature and being outdoors. "Walking in the mountains or through a quiet forest sounds amazing," she thought. Hiking would give her good exercise, allow her to enjoy fresh air, and see beautiful scenery. There are a few nice trails not far from her house.

Emily felt excited about these options. She talked to her friend, Tom, about her ideas. Tom suggested trying a bit of everything first. "Why don't you visit the library, try a simple recipe, and go for a short walk in the park? Then you can see what you like best!" Emily thought this was a great idea. She decided to try all three options next weekend before making her final decision. She believes finding a good hobby will make her weekends much more enjoyable.
