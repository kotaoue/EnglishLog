## テーマ: Healthy Eating for a Good Life (良い生活のための健康的な食事)

### 英文パッセージ

Eating healthy food is very important for all of us. It helps our bodies grow strong. It also gives us energy to study and play. We need energy every day.

What kinds of food should we eat? Fruits and vegetables are very good. They have many vitamins and minerals. These are important for our health. Try to eat different colors of fruits and vegetables every day. For example, you can eat a red apple, green broccoli, and yellow banana.

We also need to eat protein. Fish, chicken, and beans are good sources of protein. Protein helps our muscles grow. Milk and cheese have calcium. Calcium is good for our bones and teeth.

Do not forget grains like rice, bread, and pasta. They give us energy. But we should not eat too much sugar or fat. Foods like sweets and chips are okay sometimes, but not every day. Eating too much of them is not good for your health.

Drinking water is also very important. Our bodies need a lot of water. Drink water often, especially when you feel thirsty or after playing sports.

Try to eat breakfast every morning. It is the most important meal of the day. It gives you energy for school or work. Eat lunch and dinner too. Eating regularly is important.

So, let's try to eat healthy food and drink enough water. It makes our life better and helps us feel good every day.

5. この英文パッセージが一番伝えたいことは何だと思いますか？
