## テーマ: Shopping in the Future (未来の買い物)

### 英文パッセージ

How do you buy your clothes? Do you go to a big store? Today, many people shop online. They use their smartphones or computers. They can buy many things like books, toys, and food. It is very popular around the world.

Online shopping is very useful. First, you can shop at any time. Online shops are open 24 hours a day. You can buy things at midnight. Second, you do not have to go to a store. You can stay in your room. It is good for rainy days or very cold days.

How does it work? You look at pictures on a website. You choose something you like. Then, you click a button. You pay with a card or at a convenience store. A delivery person brings your box to your house. Usually, it takes two or three days. Some shops are very fast. The box arrives the next day.

But there are some problems. You cannot touch the real things. Sometimes the size of the shoes is small. Sometimes the color of the bag is different from the picture. You should read other people's comments before you buy.

Online shopping is changing our lives. It saves us a lot of time. In the future, maybe drones will fly to our houses with our boxes. Shopping will be more fun and easy for everyone.
