## テーマ: 新しい趣味で広がる世界

### 英文パッセージ

My name is Ken, and I recently started a new hobby: gardening. I used to spend a lot of time watching TV or playing video games after work. One day, my friend, Lisa, told me about her beautiful small garden. She showed me pictures of colorful flowers and fresh vegetables she grew. It looked very relaxing and rewarding.

I thought, "Why not try it?" So, I bought some simple tools, a few small pots, and some seeds for cherry tomatoes and basil. At first, it was a bit difficult. I didn't know much about soil or how often to water the plants. I read some books and watched online videos to learn. Slowly, my plants started to grow. It was exciting to see the tiny green leaves appear.

Now, every morning, I check my plants. I water them carefully and talk to them sometimes! Gardening has brought many positive changes to my life. I spend more time outdoors, which is good for my health. I also feel less stressed. When I see my cherry tomatoes turning red, I feel a sense of accomplishment. It’s also nice to cook with fresh basil I grew myself.

Lisa often visits my place to see my garden. We share tips and sometimes exchange seeds. We even plan to visit a big flower park next month. Gardening is more than just growing plants; it's a way to connect with nature and with friends. I highly recommend finding a hobby that brings you joy and helps you relax.
