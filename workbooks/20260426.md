## テーマ: 健康的な生活を送るためのヒント

### 英文パッセージ

Hello, everyone. Today, let's talk about a good life. It is important to be healthy every day.

First, try to eat healthy food. You should eat many fruits and vegetables. They are good for your body. Also, drink a lot of water. Water helps your body work well. Do not eat too much candy or fast food. These foods are sometimes tasty, but they are not always good for you.

Second, exercise regularly. You can walk, run, or play sports. Even a short walk every day is good. Exercise makes your body strong. It also makes you feel happy. Try to move your body for about 30 minutes each day.

Third, sleep well. A good sleep helps your brain and body rest. Most students need 8 to 9 hours of sleep every night. If you sleep well, you can study better and have more energy for the next day.

Lastly, relax sometimes. Life can be busy. Read a book, listen to music, or talk with your friends. These activities help you relax.

So, remember these simple tips. Eating well, exercising, sleeping enough, and relaxing are key. They can help you live a happy and healthy life. Let's try to follow these tips from today!
