## テーマ: A New Way to Work (新しい働き方)

### 英文パッセージ

Many people work at home now. They do not go to the office every day. They use computers and the internet to do their jobs. This is a new way to work. Some people call it "remote work."

There are many good things about working from home. First, you do not need to spend time on trains or buses. In the morning, you can sleep longer. You can have breakfast with your family. You have more time for your hobbies. Second, you can wear comfortable clothes. You do not need a suit or a tie every day. You can sit in your favorite chair.

But there are some bad things too. Some people feel lonely. They do not see their coworkers or friends at the office. They only talk through video meetings on the computer. Also, it is sometimes difficult to stop working. The office is in your house, so some people work very late at night. It is hard to change your mind from "work time" to "home time."

In the future, more people will work from home. Technology makes this possible. It is important to find a good balance. You should work hard during the day, but you should also rest and play. Working from home can make our lives better and more flexible if we use it well.
