## 本日の問題

## テーマ: スマートフォンが私たちの日常生活に与える影響

### 英文パッセージ

Smartphones have become an essential part of our daily lives, transforming how we communicate, learn, and entertain ourselves. It's hard to imagine a world without them today.

On the one hand, smartphones offer incredible convenience. We can instantly connect with friends and family, no matter where they are, through calls, messages, and video chats. Accessing information has never been easier; with just a few taps, we can look up anything from historical facts to cooking recipes. Moreover, these devices provide endless entertainment, such as music, movies, and games, which can make long commutes or waiting times more enjoyable. For students, smartphones can be valuable learning tools, offering educational apps and quick access to research materials.

However, there are also downsides to relying so heavily on smartphones. Many people find themselves spending too much time on their phones, which can lead to addiction and a decrease in productivity. Constant notifications and the temptation to check social media can interrupt our concentration and even cause sleep problems if we use them late at night. Furthermore, some argue that too much screen time might reduce face-to-face communication, making us less connected in person.

Therefore, it's important for us to find a balance. While smartphones offer many benefits, we should be mindful of how and when we use them. Setting limits and engaging in other activities, like reading a book or spending time with loved ones without screens, can help us enjoy the advantages of technology without letting it control our lives.
