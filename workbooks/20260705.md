## テーマ: The World of E-sports（eスポーツの世界）

### 英文パッセージ

Do you like video games? Today, playing video games is not just a hobby. It is a professional sport. We call it "e-sports." Many people around the world love e-sports. Some people play games, and many more people watch them.

Professional gamers are like traditional athletes. They practice for many hours every day. They need to have a quick brain and fast fingers. They also work in teams and make plans to win. They can earn a lot of money from prizes and sponsors. This is now a very big business.

Big e-sports events are held in large stadiums. Thousands of fans go there to see their favorite players. Millions of people also watch the games on the internet. Websites like Twitch and YouTube are very popular for this.

Why is e-sports so popular? One reason is that anyone can join. In many sports, you need to be tall or very strong. But in e-sports, your body size does not matter. You only need a computer or a game console and a lot of practice.

In the future, e-sports will become even bigger. Some people think it will be a part of the Olympic Games. It is a new and exciting world for young people. E-sports is changing how we think about sports and entertainment.
