## テーマ: 趣味を見つけて生活を豊かにする

### 英文パッセージ

Do you want to enjoy your life more? A hobby can help you. A hobby is something you like to do in your free time. It is important for your mind and body.

Many people like reading books. Reading is a good hobby. You can learn many new things from books. You can read stories about different places and people. It is easy to start reading.

Another good hobby is walking. You can walk in a park or near your home. Walking is good for your body. You can see many beautiful trees and flowers. You can feel happy and strong.

Cooking is also a fun hobby. You can make food for yourself and your family. Learning to cook is not difficult. You can try new recipes. Everyone likes delicious food.

Maybe you like music. Listening to music or playing a simple instrument can be fun. You can feel relaxed.

Find a hobby that you like. It can make your everyday life happy. You can meet new friends through your hobby, too. A hobby can bring you joy.
