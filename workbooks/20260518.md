## テーマ: My Daily Routine (私の一日のルーティン)

### 英文パッセージ

My name is Ken. I like my daily routine. I wake up at 7 o'clock every morning. This is my start of the day.
First, I eat a healthy breakfast. I usually eat toast with eggs and drink a glass of milk. Breakfast gives me energy. It is very important for my body.
After breakfast, I get ready for work. I always walk to my office. Walking is a good exercise. The office is not far from my home.
At work, I use my computer a lot. I send many emails and write reports. I also talk to my co-workers about our projects. My job is sometimes busy, but I enjoy it.
At 12:30 PM, I eat lunch. I often bring a bento from home. Sometimes, I buy a salad at a nearby cafe. Eating lunch is a good break.
In the afternoon, I continue my tasks. I sometimes have online meetings with people from other countries. English is useful then.
After work, I go straight home. I arrive home around 6:00 PM.
I like to cook dinner for myself. Tonight, I will make chicken and vegetables. Cooking is fun. Eating healthy food helps me stay strong.
After dinner, I relax. I often read a book. Reading helps me learn new words. Sometimes, I listen to music.
Before I go to bed, I prepare for tomorrow. I check my schedule. I go to bed at 10:30 PM. I sleep for about 7.5 hours.
Getting enough sleep is very important for my health. My daily routine helps me to be happy and healthy. I try to keep this routine every day.
