## テーマ: 健康的な朝の習慣

### 英文パッセージ

Good morning! A good day often starts with good habits. I want to tell you about my morning routine. It helps me feel happy and strong.

First, I usually get up early, around 6:30 AM. This gives me plenty of time. I do not rush. Next, I drink a big glass of water. Water is very important for our bodies. It wakes me up.

Then, I eat a healthy breakfast. My favorite breakfast is yogurt with fruit and a slice of toast. Eating breakfast gives me energy to start my work or study. It is like fuel for my body.

After breakfast, I sometimes go for a short walk outside. The fresh air feels great. Walking for 15-20 minutes is a simple exercise. It makes my body active. I also try to read a newspaper or a small book for about 10 minutes. This helps my mind.

These small actions are not difficult, but they are very important for my health. They help me focus better and feel less stressed during the day.

It is also very important to sleep well at night. I try to sleep for about 7 to 8 hours. When I get enough sleep, I feel fresh and ready for new challenges in the morning.

Having a good routine helps me manage my day better. It makes me feel productive and happy. Try to find some good habits for your morning. You will see a positive change in your life. Start small, be consistent, and enjoy your healthy new day!
