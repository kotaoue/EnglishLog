## テーマ: Akiの一日 (Aki's Day)

### 英文パッセージ

My name is Aki. I am a junior high school student. I live in a small town near the mountains. It is a quiet place.
Every morning, I wake up at 6:30. I eat breakfast with my family. My mother makes good food for us. She is a great cook. I like toast and milk.
Then, I go to school. I walk to school. It takes about 15 minutes. My friend, Hana, walks with me. We talk about many things on the way.
School starts at 8:30. I study many subjects like Japanese, math, science, and English. I like math and English. English is a little difficult for me, but I try my best to learn new words and grammar. Our English teacher is Mr. Green. He is a nice person and helps us a lot.
At 12:00, we eat lunch. I usually bring my lunch from home. Today, I have rice balls and some fruit. Hana has a sandwich and a juice. We eat and talk in the classroom.
In the afternoon, we have more classes. Sometimes, we have physical education. I like playing basketball with my friends. It is very fun.
School finishes at 3:30. After school, I often go to the library. I like to read books. Today, I read a book about animals and nature. It is very interesting to learn new things.
Then, I go home. I do my homework in my room. My room is small but cozy. I always keep it clean.
In the evening, I eat dinner with my family. We talk about our day and laugh a lot. My father works at a company in the city. My mother works at home and takes care of our family.
After dinner, I watch TV or listen to music. I like pop music and sometimes play my guitar.
I go to bed at 10:00 PM. This is my typical day from Monday to Friday. It is busy, but I am happy with my life.
