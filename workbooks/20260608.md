## テーマ: Pets in Our Lives (私たちの生活の中のペット)

### 英文パッセージ

Pets are very special friends for many people. They live with us in our homes. Many families have pets. Dogs and cats are two very popular kinds of pets. Dogs love to play outside and go for walks. Cats like to sleep in warm places and play with toys. Some people also have small birds, colorful fish, or cute rabbits as pets.

Pets bring much joy and happiness to our lives. When we play with them, we feel good. A dog wags its tail when it sees you. A cat makes a happy sound when you pet it. These small things make us smile. We can talk to our pets, and they seem to understand us. They are always there for us.

Taking care of pets is a big responsibility. We must give them healthy food and fresh water every day. We also need to clean their spaces to keep them healthy. For dogs, daily walks are important. For all pets, love and attention are very important. If our pets get sick, we must take them to a vet, an animal doctor.

Pets teach us many important lessons. They teach us about love, kindness, and how to care for others. They show us how to be responsible. Our pets are not just animals; they are like family members. They make our homes warm and full of life. Loving a pet is a wonderful thing. We should always treat them well.
