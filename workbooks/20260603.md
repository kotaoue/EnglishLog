## テーマ: 新しい学びの形：オンライン学習

### 英文パッセージ

Online learning is a new way to study. Many students around the world use it. You can learn many things online. For example, you can study English, math, or history. You can also learn how to play the piano or how to cook.

One good thing about online learning is that you can study anywhere. You do not need to go to a school building. You can learn at your home, in a park, or even in a cafe. All you need is a computer or a tablet and the internet. This makes learning very easy and fun.

Another good thing is that you can study any time. If you are busy in the morning, you can study in the evening. If you want to review a lesson, you can watch the video again and again. You can learn at your own speed. This helps you understand difficult topics better.

Online learning also lets you connect with many people. You can talk to teachers and students from different countries. This is a great chance to learn about new cultures and make new friends. You can share your ideas and ask questions.

Many people think online learning is important for the future. It gives everyone a chance to learn new skills and get new knowledge. It is a good tool for students, workers, and even old people. Learning never stops with online tools. It helps us grow every day. So, let's try online learning!

    - あなたの回答: どこでもできる。学校の建物も必要とせず家でも公園でもカフェでも、コンピューターとかタブレットがインターネットに繋がれば学習できる

5. この英文の筆者が最も伝えたいことは何だと思いますか？
