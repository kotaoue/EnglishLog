## テーマ: My Day and Technology

### 英文パッセージ

Hello. My name is Ken. I am a student. I wake up at 7 o'clock every morning. My alarm clock is on my smartphone. It rings loudly. After I wake up, I check my phone. I read news on the internet. This helps me know about the world. I eat breakfast with my family. Then, I go to school.

At school, we sometimes use computers. We learn English and math. Computers help us learn new things. Teachers show us pictures and videos. This makes learning fun.

After school, I come home. I sometimes play games on my tablet. My tablet is like a big smartphone. It is fun to play games with my friends online. We talk and laugh. But I do not play games for a long time. I also do my homework. Sometimes, I use my computer for homework. I search for information on the internet. My computer helps me write reports.

In the evening, my family watches TV. We watch movies or news programs. TV is a kind of technology. It brings information and entertainment to our home. Before I sleep, I sometimes read a book. Or I listen to music on my phone.

Technology is a big part of my life. Smartphones, computers, tablets, and TV are all around me. They help me learn, play, and connect with people. It is important to use them well. I try to use them for good things. I also make time for other things, like playing outside or talking with my family. My life is busy but fun.
