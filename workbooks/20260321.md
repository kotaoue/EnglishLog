## テーマ: 良い一日のための健康習慣

### 英文パッセージ

Good morning! How are you today? Let's talk about healthy habits. They are very important for a good day. Healthy habits help our body and our mind.

First, eat a good breakfast. It gives you energy for the morning. Try to eat fruit and vegetables. They are good for your health. Please do not eat too much sugar. Too much sugar is not good for your body.

Second, drink water often. Our body needs water every day. It helps us feel good and stay active. Drink water before you feel thirsty. Keep a small bottle of water near you.

Third, move your body. You can walk to school or run in the park. Playing sports with friends is also a good idea. Exercise makes your body strong. It also makes your mind happy. Don't sit too long.

Fourth, get enough sleep. Sleep is very important for everyone. Our body rests when we sleep. We need about seven to eight hours of sleep every night. Go to bed at the same time every day.

Fifth, relax your mind. Sometimes, we feel stressed. Listen to music or draw a picture. Reading a fun book is also good. Take a deep breath. These things help you feel calm.

These simple habits are easy to start today. Choose one habit and try it. Your good day starts with good habits. Let's be happy and healthy together!
