## テーマ: The Importance of Sleep (睡眠の重要性)

### 英文パッセージ

Sleep is very important for our body and mind. We need good sleep every night. If we sleep well, we can feel strong and happy. Our body can rest and get new energy. Our brain also needs rest. When we sleep, our brain works to fix things. It helps us remember new things. It helps us learn better at school or work.

Many people do not get enough sleep. This can make us feel tired during the day. We might make mistakes. We might feel sad or angry. It is important to get 7 to 9 hours of sleep. This is good for most adults. Children and teenagers need even more sleep.

What can we do to sleep better? First, try to go to bed at the same time every night. This helps our body. Second, do not use your phone or computer right before bed. The light from screens can make it hard to sleep. Third, try to read a book or listen to quiet music. This can relax your mind. Fourth, do not drink coffee or strong tea in the evening. These drinks have caffeine. Caffeine can keep you awake.

Good sleep is like good food and exercise. It is a key part of a healthy life. We can all try to sleep better. It will make our days brighter. It will make us feel better. Let's make sleep a priority. Our health will thank us.
