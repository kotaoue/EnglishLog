## 本日の問題

## テーマ: 私たちのスマートフォン

### 英文パッセージ

Many people have a smartphone today. It is a small computer. We can put it in our pocket and take it anywhere. My smartphone is a very useful tool. I use it every day for many things.

First, I use it to talk to my friends and family. I can send messages to them. I can also call them. My phone helps me to stay in touch with important people.

Next, I often use my phone to take pictures. My phone has a good camera. I like to take photos of beautiful places and my happy moments. I can save them on my phone.

I also like to watch videos. There are many fun videos on the internet. I can watch them anytime, anywhere. Sometimes, I read e-books on my phone too. It is easy to carry many books in one small device.

My phone helps me with my studies. When I don't know a word, I can use a dictionary app. I can also search for information about my homework. It is a good study partner.

My father uses his phone for his work. He sends emails and talks to his colleagues. My sister uses her phone to listen to music. She likes pop songs very much. We all use our phones in different ways.

But it is important to use it wisely. My mother always says, "Do not look at your phone all the time." Looking at a small screen for a long time is not good for your eyes.

I try to follow her words. I spend time with my family and friends face to face. I also like to go outside. I can play sports or walk in the park. Smartphones are good, but real life is also good. It is important to have a good balance.

### 設問

1. 筆者はスマートフォンを使って誰と連絡を取っていますか？
    - あなたの回答: 友達

2. 筆者はスマートフォンで他にどのようなことをすると述べていますか？（2つ挙げてください）
    - あなたの回答: 動画を見る。辞書アプリで検索する

3. 筆者の家族の中で、お父さんとお姉さんはスマートフォンをそれぞれどのように使っていますか？
    - あなたの回答: お父さんは仕事。お姉さんは音楽を聞く。

4. パッセージ中の "useful tool" という表現は、どのような意味ですか？
    - あなたの回答: 便利なツール

5. このパッセージで筆者が最も伝えたいことは何ですか？
    - あなたの回答: 自分と家族のスマホの使い方
