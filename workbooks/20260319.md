## 本日の問題

## テーマ: サラさんの楽しい週末

### 英文パッセージ

Sarah loves her weekends. After a busy week at work, she always looks forward to Saturday and Sunday. On Saturday mornings, she usually wakes up early, around 7:00 AM. First, she makes a cup of coffee and reads a book for about an hour. Reading is one of her favorite hobbies. She often reads fantasy novels or historical fiction.

After reading, she goes to a local park to exercise. She enjoys jogging and sometimes joins a yoga class there. The park is beautiful with many trees and a small pond, so she feels refreshed after her workout. In the afternoon, Sarah often meets her friends for lunch. They try different restaurants in the city. Last Saturday, they went to a new Italian place and had delicious pasta. In the evening, she might watch a movie at home or go to a concert.

Sunday is a bit different. Sarah likes to relax more. She usually spends time with her family. They often cook together or visit a museum. Sometimes, they go on a short trip to the countryside. Sarah believes that spending time with loved ones is very important. She also uses Sunday afternoon to prepare for the upcoming week. She plans her meals and organizes her schedule. This helps her feel ready for Monday morning. Sarah always tries to make the most of her weekends, balancing relaxation, exercise, social activities, and family time.
