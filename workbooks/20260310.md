## 本日の問題

## テーマ: My Daily Life

### 英文パッセージ

Hi! My name is Alex. I am a student. I go to school every day. I wake up at 7:00 AM. I eat breakfast with my family. We eat bread and drink milk. After breakfast, I go to school. School starts at 8:30 AM.

I have many classes. My favorite class is English. I like to read and write. After school, I go home. I do my homework. Then, I play with my friends. We play in the park.

In the evening, I eat dinner with my family again. We talk about our day. After dinner, I watch TV. I like cartoons. I go to bed at 9:00 PM. I read a book before I sleep. I like my daily life! It is fun and busy. I am happy.

On weekends, I do not go to school. I relax at home. I play games and read books. I sometimes go to the zoo with my family. I like to see the animals. Weekends are my favorite!
