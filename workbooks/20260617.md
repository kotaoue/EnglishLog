## テーマ: The Future of Cars (車の未来)

### 英文パッセージ

Do you like cars? Many people use cars every day. We go to school or work by car. But most cars use gasoline. Gasoline makes the air dirty. This is bad for the earth and for our health.

Now, electric cars are becoming very popular. These cars do not use gasoline. They use electricity. They have large batteries inside. You can charge the battery at your house or at a station. Electric cars are very quiet. They do not make a big noise. Driving them is very smooth and easy.

Modern cars have a lot of new technology. They use computers and cameras. These things help the driver. In the future, we will have self-driving cars. A self-driving car can drive without a person. You can talk to your friends or read a book in the car. It is like a moving room.

Many companies are making electric cars now. The price of these cars is a little high now. But many people think the price will be lower in the future. Clean air is very important for our world. We want to protect nature and the animals. Electric cars are good for our green earth. Technology changes our life and makes it better. Let’s think about the future of cars together.
