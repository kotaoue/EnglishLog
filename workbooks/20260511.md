## テーマ: 健康的な朝食の重要性

### 英文パッセージ

Morning is an important time. You wake up and start a new day. Eating breakfast is a very good habit. It gives you energy for the day. Your brain needs energy to work well at school or work. Your body also needs energy to move, run, and play sports.

What kind of food is good for breakfast? Fruits are excellent. Apples, bananas, and oranges have many vitamins. They help your body stay healthy and strong. Vegetables like spinach can be good too, often in a smoothie. Eggs are also a great choice. They have protein. Protein helps your body build muscles and feel full. Milk or yogurt is good for strong bones. They have calcium, a very important mineral.

It is not good to eat too much sugar in the morning. Sweet drinks or sugary cereals can make you feel tired later in the day. Instead, choose foods like whole-wheat bread or oats. They give you energy slowly over time. This helps you feel full and focused for a long time, until lunchtime.

Eating breakfast every day is a simple step for a healthy life. It helps you have a better day. You can study better in class. You can play better with friends. So, remember: do not skip breakfast! It is truly the most important meal of the day for your health.
