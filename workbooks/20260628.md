## テーマ: AI in Our Lives（私たちの生活の中のAI）

### 英文パッセージ

AI is short for Artificial Intelligence. Many people use AI every day. It is in our phones, cars, and computers. AI helps us in many ways.

When you talk to your phone, AI listens to you. It understands your voice and answers your questions. It can tell you the weather or play your favorite music. AI is also useful for learning. Some people use AI to study English. AI can find mistakes in your sentences. It can give you advice on how to write better.

In the future, AI will become smarter. Cars will drive themselves with the help of AI. This will make our lives safer and easier. However, some people worry about AI. They think AI might take their jobs. But AI is just a tool. It is made to help humans. We need to learn how to use it well.

If we use AI correctly, we can solve many problems. For example, it can help doctors find illnesses early. It can also help us save energy in our homes. AI is a big part of our future. We should try to understand it more. Let's work together with AI for a better world.
