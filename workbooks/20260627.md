## テーマ: The Future of Food (未来の食べ物)

### 英文パッセージ

Food is very important for all of us. Every day, we eat breakfast, lunch, and dinner. But the world is changing. There are more and more people on Earth every year. By the year 2050, there will be about 10 billion people. We need to find a way to give food to everyone.

Today, many scientists are working on the future of food. One interesting idea is "lab-grown meat." Scientists make this meat in a laboratory. We do not need to keep many cows or chickens for this. This is good for nature. To make regular meat, we need a lot of water and big farms. Lab-grown meat uses less water and land.

Another idea is eating insects. In some countries, people already eat crickets or bees. Insects have a lot of protein. They are very healthy for our bodies. Also, they do not need much food or space to grow. They are better for the Earth than cows or pigs.

Technology is also changing how we cook. Some people use 3D printers to make food. You can put healthy ingredients into the printer. Then, it makes a meal with a beautiful shape. You can make food that is easy to eat for old people or children.

Our meals in the future might be very different from today. A burger made in a lab or a snack made of insects might be normal. These new ideas help us protect the Earth and feed everyone. It is an exciting time for food.
