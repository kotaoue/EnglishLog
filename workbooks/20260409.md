## テーマ: My Morning Routine (私の朝のルーティン)

### 英文パッセージ

I wake up at 6:00 AM every day. My alarm clock rings loudly. I get out of bed quickly. First, I go to the bathroom. I wash my face and brush my teeth. This makes me feel fresh and ready. Then I go to the kitchen. I make breakfast for myself. Usually, I eat toast and drink milk. Sometimes I eat fruit, like an apple or an orange. It is a healthy breakfast. After breakfast, I get dressed. I choose my clothes for school. I like to wear comfortable clothes. Then I pack my school bag. I check if I have all my books and my pen case. I also put my lunch box in my bag. Sometimes I listen to music while I get ready. Music makes me feel good and happy. I leave home at 7:30 AM. I walk to school every day. My school is not very far from my house. On the way, I often see my friends. We walk together and talk about many things. School starts at 8:00 AM. My morning is busy, but it is a good start for my day. I like my morning routine very much. It helps me prepare for school and feel positive.

### 設問

1. 筆者は毎日何時に起きますか？
   - あなたの回答: 6:00

2. 筆者は朝食に何をよく食べますか？（2つ挙げてください）
   - あなたの回答: トーストとミルクの組み合わせ/フルーツ

3. 筆者はどのように学校へ行きますか？
   - あなたの回答: 歩いて

4. 英文中にある「This makes me feel fresh and ready.」の "fresh" は、筆者がどのような気持ちになったことを表していますか？
   - あなたの回答: やるきがあってスッキリした感じ

5. 筆者はなぜ自分の朝のルーティンを「とても好き (very much)」だと言っていますか？
   - あなたの回答: 学校でいい感じに過ごすことの準備になるから
