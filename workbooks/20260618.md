## テーマ: The Power of the Internet (インターネットの力)

### 英文パッセージ

The internet is very important in our lives today. Almost everyone uses it every day. We can do many things with it. For example, we can talk to our friends. We use email or social media to say hello. Sometimes we use video calls to see their faces. This is very good for people who live far away. You can feel close to your family even if they are in another country.

We also use the internet to learn new things. If you have a question, you can search for the answer immediately. You can find many websites about history, science, or English. You can also watch videos to learn how to cook or play the guitar. The internet is like a big library that never closes. It is open twenty-four hours a day.

Shopping is another popular thing to do. We can buy clothes, books, and food online. It is very easy because we do not have to leave our house. We can look at many shops at the same time. This saves us a lot of time. In business, many people work using the internet too. They send files and have meetings online. This means people can work from home or anywhere they like.

However, we must be careful. We should not use the internet for a very long time. It is important to talk to people face-to-face too. Also, we must protect our personal information. Do not tell your password to others. In conclusion, the internet makes our lives better and more convenient. If we use it in a good way, we can learn a lot and stay happy.
