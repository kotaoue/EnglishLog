## テーマ: Our Beautiful Planet: Protecting Nature

### 英文パッセージ

Our Earth is a beautiful planet. We can see green forests and tall mountains. Blue oceans and clean rivers are also here. Many animals live in nature. Birds fly in the sky. Fish swim in the water. We love our beautiful world.

Nature is very important for us. It gives us fresh air to breathe. We get clean water to drink. Food like fruits and vegetables comes from nature. Animals also need nature to live. Nature is our big home.

But now, our planet has some problems. There is too much trash. Plastic bottles and bags are everywhere. This trash makes our rivers and oceans dirty. It is pollution. Many animals are in danger because of this.

We must help our planet. We can do small things every day. For example, we can reduce our trash. We can reuse bags and recycle paper. Planting trees is also a good idea. Trees give us fresh air. We can save electricity at home.

Every person can make a difference. Small actions from many people can make our Earth clean and healthy again. Let's protect our beautiful nature for our future.
