## 本日の問題

## テーマ: ケンの楽しい一日

### 英文パッセージ

Hi, my name is Ken. I am a student. I am 13 years old. I live in Tokyo.

Every day, my morning starts at 7:00. I wake up, and then I brush my teeth. After that, I eat breakfast. My mother makes toast and eggs. I drink milk and orange juice.

At 8:00, I leave my house for school. I walk to school with my friend, Lisa. We talk about many things. School starts at 8:30. We study many subjects. My favorite subject is English. Our English teacher is kind.

At 12:00, we have lunch. I usually eat a packed lunch. Lisa buys lunch at the school cafeteria. Her lunch looks good. In the afternoon, we have more classes. Sometimes, I have P.E. class. I like sports like soccer and basketball.

School finishes at 3:30. After school, I often go to the library. I read books there. I like adventure stories. Sometimes, I play soccer with my friends in the park near my house. It is very fun.

I go home at 5:00. Then, I do my homework. It takes about one hour. Math homework is sometimes difficult. At 7:00, my family eats dinner together. My father, mother, and my little sister, Hana, are there. We eat tasty Japanese food. We talk about our day. My sister tells funny stories.

After dinner, I watch TV for a short time. My favorite TV program starts at 8:00. Before bed, I take a bath. It is relaxing. I go to bed at 10:00. I sleep well. This is my normal day. It is a busy but happy day for me. I love my family and friends.

### 設問

1. ケンは何時に朝食を食べますか？
    - あなたの回答: 7:00

2. ケンが学校で一番好きな科目は何ですか？
    - あなたの回答: 英語

3. ケンは放課後に何をすることがありますか？2つ答えてください。
    - あなたの回答: 図書館に行って本を読む。友達と家の近くの公園でサッカーをする

4. パッセージの中で「My mother makes toast and eggs.」とありますが、「makes」はここではどのような意味で使われていますか？
    - あなたの回答: 料理をする

5. この英文の主な目的は何ですか？
    - あなたの回答: ケンの平日の1日を紹介

### 単語問題

1. 「subject」という単語は、この文脈（We study many subjects.）でどのような意味で使われていますか？
    - あなたの回答: 科目

2. 「relaxing」という単語は、この文脈（It is relaxing.）でどのような気持ちや状態を表していますか？
    - あなたの回答: リラックスして落ち着いた気分
