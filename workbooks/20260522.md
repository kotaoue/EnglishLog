## テーマ: Healthy Eating for a Happy Life

### 英文パッセージ

Eating healthy food is very important for our body. It gives us energy to work and play every day. Good food helps us to be strong and happy.

What is healthy food? Fruits and vegetables are very good for us. They have many vitamins. These vitamins help our body stay well. Meat and fish also give us protein. Protein helps our muscles grow strong. We also need some bread, rice, or pasta for more energy. These foods are called carbohydrates.

Drinking enough water every day is also important. Water helps all parts of our body work well. We should drink at least 6 to 8 glasses of water.

But some food is not very good for us. Food with too much sugar or salt can be bad for our health. For example, many candies and sweet drinks have a lot of sugar. Fast food is often not healthy either. It may have too much fat and oil. We can eat these foods sometimes, but not every day.

So, try to choose fresh food. You can buy fruits and vegetables at a store. Cook at home if you can. Eating well makes you feel good and think well. It is a good habit for a long and happy life.
