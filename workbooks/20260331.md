## テーマ: ケンさんの健康的な毎日の習慣

### 英文パッセージ

Hello. My name is Ken. I want to tell you about my good daily habits.
Every morning, I wake up early. I like to see the morning sun.
First, I drink a big glass of water. Water is very important for my body. It makes me feel fresh.
Then, I eat a healthy breakfast. Breakfast gives me energy for the day. I often eat fruit, like an apple, and some bread. Fruit is sweet and good for health.
After breakfast, I go outside. I like to walk in the park near my house. Walking is a simple and good exercise. It makes my legs strong. I see many green trees and colorful flowers there. The air is clean.
In the afternoon, I sometimes read a book. Reading is fun and I can learn new things. Sometimes I listen to music. Music makes me happy.
In the evening, I eat dinner with my family. We talk about our day.
Before I go to bed, I always brush my teeth. This is important for my teeth and mouth.
I try to sleep for about eight hours every night. Good sleep is very important for my body and mind. It helps me feel good the next day.
These simple habits help me to stay healthy and happy every day. They are not difficult. You can try them too.
