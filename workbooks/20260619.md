## テーマ: Living in a Smart Home (スマートホームでの暮らし)

### 英文パッセージ

Welcome to the world of smart homes. A smart home uses technology to make our lives very easy. In this house, many things are connected to the internet. We can control them with a smartphone or a computer.

Imagine your morning. When you wake up, the lights become bright slowly. The coffee machine starts to make your favorite coffee automatically. You do not need to push any buttons. You can stay in bed for five more minutes.

A smart speaker is usually in the living room. You can talk to it like a friend. For example, you can say, "Please play some happy music," or "What is the weather like today?" The speaker answers your questions immediately. It can also read the news for you while you eat breakfast.

You can check your house even when you are not there. Use your smartphone to see the camera in the kitchen. You can lock the doors or turn off the air conditioner from your office or a train. This makes us feel very safe and comfortable.

Smart homes help us save energy and money, too. The lights turn off when no one is in the room. The heater knows when you are coming home and starts at the right time. This is good for the Earth and our life.

However, some people worry about their privacy. They think the internet knows too much about their daily life. We must use technology carefully. But technology is getting better and safer every day.

In the future, many more people will live in smart homes. It is a new and exciting way of living. It saves us a lot of time and helps us relax.
