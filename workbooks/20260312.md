## テーマ: My First Day at School

### 英文パッセージ

Hi, everyone! My name is Hana, and I’m 13 years old. Today was my first day at junior high school. I was very nervous, but also excited.

I woke up early and put on my new school uniform. It was a little big, but I liked it. I ate breakfast with my family. My mom made me a special lunchbox with my favorite food: onigiri!

When I arrived at school, there were so many students. I didn't know anyone. I felt a little scared. But then, a girl with a big smile came up to me. She said, "Hi! I'm Yumi. Are you new here?"

I said, "Yes, I'm Hana." Yumi was very friendly. She showed me around the school. We went to our classroom together. My classmates were also very nice.

In class, we introduced ourselves. The teacher, Mr. Tanaka, was very kind. He told us about the school rules and the subjects we would study.

After school, Yumi and I walked home together. We talked about our favorite things. I learned that she likes to draw, and I like to read books. We promised to meet again tomorrow.

My first day at junior high school was great! I made a new friend, and I'm looking forward to learning new things. I'm happy I came to this school.

### 設問

1.  ハナは何歳ですか？
   - あなたの回答: 13歳

2.  ハナの母親は、彼女のためにどんなお弁当を作りましたか？
   - あなたの回答: おにぎり

3.  ハナは学校で誰と出会いましたか？その人はハナに何をしてくれましたか？
   - あなたの回答: ユミという女の子に出会いました。ユミは学校を案内してくれました。

4.  英文中の "nervous" はどのような意味ですか？
   - あなたの回答: 不安な、緊張している

5.  この英文の主な目的は何ですか？
   - あなたの回答: ハナが中学校の初日について語り、その経験を読者に伝えること。
