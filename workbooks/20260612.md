## テーマ: Recycling for a Better World

### 英文パッセージ

Recycling is very important for our earth. It means we take old things and make new things from them. This helps our environment. We can recycle many things in our daily life.

Paper is one common thing. You can recycle old newspapers, magazines, and cardboard boxes. These go to a special factory. The factory makes new paper from them. This saves many trees. Trees give us fresh air, so saving trees is very good.

Plastic is another thing we can recycle. Many plastic bottles and containers can be recycled. They are used to make new plastic items. It is important to clean them before you put them in the recycling bin.

Glass bottles and metal cans are also recyclable. After people use them, they can be cleaned and sent to a factory. The factory melts them down and makes new bottles or cans. This saves a lot of energy.

Why is recycling important? First, it helps to reduce the amount of garbage. Less garbage means cleaner land and water. Second, it saves natural resources. We don't need to cut down so many new trees or dig for new metals. Third, it saves energy. Making things from recycled materials often uses less energy than making them from new materials.

What can we do? We can learn what to recycle in our town. We can separate our garbage into different bins for paper, plastic, and glass. We can also buy products made from recycled materials. This simple action helps our planet. Let's recycle every day for a better future.
