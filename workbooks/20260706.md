## テーマ: Traveling to Space (宇宙旅行)

### 英文パッセージ

Space tourism is an exciting topic today. Long ago, only special people called astronauts went to space. They worked very hard and stayed in space for a long time. Now, things are changing. Some big companies are making new rockets. They want to take normal people to space. This is called space tourism.

Today, a trip to space costs a lot of money. Most people cannot pay for it. Only very rich people can go now. They fly high and see the Earth for a few minutes. Then they come back to the ground. In the future, many people think the price will become cheaper. More people will have the chance to fly.

What do people do in space? The most famous thing is floating. In space, there is no gravity. You do not stay on the floor. You can move in the air like a bird. This is very fun and strange. Also, you can see the Earth. The Earth looks like a blue ball in the dark sky. Many travelers say it is the most beautiful view in their lives.

There are some problems too. Rockets need a lot of fuel. This can be bad for the air and the Earth. Also, space is very dangerous. Companies must make sure the rockets are safe. Scientists are working hard to solve these problems. Space travel will become a normal thing in the future. We will visit the moon or stay in space hotels one day.
