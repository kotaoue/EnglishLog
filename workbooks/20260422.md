## テーマ: 健康的な生活のヒント

### 英文パッセージ

Living a healthy life is very important for all of us. It helps us to feel good and strong every day. There are some simple things we can do.

First, we should eat good food. Fruits like apples and oranges are healthy. Vegetables like carrots and tomatoes are also good. Try to eat many colors of vegetables. Eating balanced meals gives our body energy. We should also drink a lot of water. Water helps our body work well.

Second, exercise is key. Moving our body makes us strong. You can walk in a park, run for a short time, or play a sport. Even a quick walk every day can help. Exercise is good for our body and our mind. It can make us happy.

Third, getting enough sleep is necessary. Most adults need about 7 to 8 hours of sleep each night. Children and teenagers need more. When we sleep, our body rests and gets ready for the next day. A good sleep makes us think clearly.

Finally, try to relax. Our life can be busy. Sometimes, we feel stress. Reading a book, listening to calm music, or spending time with friends can help. These activities make our mind peaceful.

These healthy habits are not difficult. If we do them every day, we can have a happy and strong life.

5. この英文の主な目的は何だと思いますか？
