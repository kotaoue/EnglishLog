## テーマ: The Power of Music (音楽の力)

### 英文パッセージ

Music is a big part of our lives. We hear music everywhere, like on TV, on the radio, or on our phones. It is more than just sounds. Music has a special power.

First, music can change how we feel. When you are sad, you can listen to fast, happy music. This can make you feel better. When you want to relax, slow and calm music is good. It can make you feel peaceful. So, music is like a friend for our feelings. It helps us deal with many emotions.

Second, music helps us remember things. Do you have a song that reminds you of a special day? Maybe it was a trip or a party. When you hear that song again, you can remember that time clearly. Music can store our memories well. It helps us recall good times and even learn new information.

Third, some people use music to focus. When you study or work, soft music can help you concentrate. It can make your brain calm and ready to learn. Many students listen to instrumental music because it does not have words that might distract them. But be careful, sometimes loud music can make it hard to focus.

Also, music connects people. When people listen to the same song, they can feel happy together. At concerts, many people sing and dance. They share joy. Music can bring people closer, even if they don't speak the same language. It is a universal language that everyone can understand.

In conclusion, music is very powerful. It can make us happy, help us remember, and connect us. It is a wonderful gift. Let's enjoy music every day and feel its power in our lives.

### 設問

1. パッセージによると、悲しい時に音楽はどのように私たちを助けてくれますか？
    - あなたの回答: まるで友達のように助けてくれて、ちょっといいなという感じになるように助けてくれる

2. 音楽が「記憶」に良い影響を与えるのはどのような時ですか？
    - あなたの回答: 特別なときの記憶が音楽と一緒に記憶されるから、記憶する時と思い出す時

3. 勉強中に音楽を聴くことについて、パッセージは何と述べていますか？良い点と注意点をそれぞれ答えてください。
    - あなたの回答: 集中力を高める役に立つ、うるさい音楽は集中しづらくなる

4. パッセージに出てくる「universal language」という表現は、音楽についてどのようなことを伝えようとしていますか？
    - あなたの回答: 一種の誰でも理解できる言語という側面

5. この英文パッセージの主な目的は何ですか？
    - あなたの回答: 音楽の魅力

    - あなたの回答: 力

2. 3つ目の段落にある "distract" はどういう意味ですか？
