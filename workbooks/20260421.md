## 本日の問題

## テーマ: 私の新しい友達、ケン

### 英文パッセージ

Hello. My name is Kota. I am a student. I am 13 years old. I live in Tokyo. I like to read books. I have a new friend. His name is Ken. Ken is also 13 years old. He is a student, too. Ken lives near my house. We go to the same school.

Ken is good at sports. He plays soccer very well. I like to watch his games. We often play together after school. Sometimes we go to the park. We kick a ball there. Sometimes we read books at my house. Ken likes science books. I like story books.

Today is Saturday. Ken and I went to the library. We looked for new books. Ken found a book about space. I found a book about animals. After the library, we went to a cafe. We drank orange juice. We talked about our favorite things. Ken wants to be a scientist. I want to be a writer. We had a very good time.

Ken is a very kind friend. He always helps me. If I don't understand homework, he teaches me. I help him sometimes, too. We learn many things from each other. I am happy to have Ken as my friend. He makes my school life fun. Friendship is important for us.

### 設問

1. コウタの新しい友達の名前は何ですか？
    - あなたの回答: ケン

2. ケンはどんな種類のスポーツが得意ですか？
    - あなたの回答: サッカー

3. コウタとケンは図書館に行った後、どこへ行きましたか？
    - あなたの回答: カフェ

4. パッセージの中で「kind」という単語が出てきます。これはどのような意味ですか？
    - あなたの回答: 親切な

5. このパッセージは主に何について書かれていますか？
    - あなたの回答: 新しい友達のケンについて
