## テーマ: 私の新しい趣味「ウクレレ」

### 英文パッセージ

Hello everyone. My name is Mei. Today, I want to talk about my new hobby. I started learning to play the ukulele a few months ago. I always wanted to play a musical instrument, but I thought it was too difficult. One day, I saw a friend playing the ukulele, and it looked so fun and easy! So, I decided to try it.

First, I bought a small ukulele online. It was not very expensive. Then, I found many free lesson videos on YouTube. I started practicing for about 30 minutes every evening after school. At first, my fingers hurt a little, and it was hard to make the right sounds. But I didn't give up. My friend also helped me sometimes.

Now, I can play a few simple songs. I often play my favorite pop songs and some folk songs. Playing the ukulele makes me feel very relaxed and happy. It's a great way to forget about my homework and school stress. I also like to sing along while playing. My family enjoys listening to my music, too.

I think learning a new instrument is a wonderful experience. It teaches me patience and how to practice consistently. If you are looking for a new hobby, I highly recommend trying the ukulele. It's portable, affordable, and brings a lot of joy! Maybe you can start your own musical journey soon.

### 設問

1. メイは最近どんな新しい趣味を始めましたか？
   - あなたの回答: ウクレレの演奏

2. メイがウクレレを始めたきっかけは何でしたか？
   - あなたの回答: 友達がとても楽しく簡単にウクレレを弾いているのを見たから

3. メイはウクレレの練習をどのように始めましたか？
   - あなたの回答: オンラインでウクレレを買って、YouTubeで無料のレッスン動画を見て始めた。

4. 英文中で "give up" という表現が出てきますが、これはどのような意味で使われていますか？
   - あなたの回答: 諦める

5. この英文の主な目的は何だと思いますか？
   - あなたの回答: 新しく始めた趣味のウクレレの魅力を紹介

### 単語問題

1. 英文中の「musical instrument」はどのようなものを指しますか？
   - あなたの回答: ミュージカルのBGM

2. 英文中の「portable」という単語は、ウクレレのどのような特徴を表していますか？
   - あなたの回答: ギターよりも小さくて持ち運びしやすい
