## テーマ: 健康的な生活

### 英文パッセージ

Living a healthy life is important for everyone. It makes us happy and strong. What do we need for a healthy life?

First, we need good food. We should eat many vegetables and fruits. They have good vitamins. We should also drink a lot of water every day. Water is very important for our body.

Second, we need to move our body. Sports are good. You can play soccer or run in the park. Walking is also a good exercise. You can walk to school or walk with your dog. Exercise makes your body strong and helps you sleep well.

Third, sleep is very important. We need to sleep 7 or 8 hours every night. When we sleep, our body rests and gets ready for a new day. If you do not sleep enough, you may feel tired.

Also, having fun is good for our mind. You can read books, listen to music, or meet friends. Laughing with friends makes you happy. These things help reduce stress. Stress is not good for our health.

So, remember these things. Eat good food, do exercise, sleep well, and have fun. A healthy life is a happy life.
