## テーマ: Learning Online (オンラインで学ぶこと)

### 英文パッセージ

Many students now study online. They do not go to school buildings every day. Instead, they use computers and tablets at home. This way of learning is very popular now. Students can study at any time they like. They watch videos of their teachers. If a lesson is difficult, they can stop the video. They can watch it many times. This helps them understand the lesson well.

Online learning also connects people. Students can talk to friends in other countries. They use the internet to see each other's faces. They talk about their lives, food, and hobbies in English. It is very exciting to have friends around the world.

However, some students miss their schools. They want to play sports with friends in the school yard. They want to eat lunch together in the classroom. School is a place to make friends and talk to them face to face.

So, which is better? Many people think both are important. In the future, students will use both schools and the internet. They will go to school some days a week. They will study at home on other days. Technology makes our lives very convenient. We can learn many things from our homes. The world is like one big classroom now. We can keep learning every day.
