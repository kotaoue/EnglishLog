## テーマ: The World of Digital Books (電子書籍の世界)

### 英文パッセージ

Many people love reading books. In the past, people only read paper books. But now, many people use e-books. E-books are digital books. You can read them on your computer, tablet, or smartphone.

Why do people like e-books? There are many reasons. First, e-books are very light. You can carry hundreds of books in one small device. You do not need a big, heavy bag. You can read your favorite stories on the bus or at the park easily.

Second, you can buy e-books anytime. You do not need to go to a bookstore. You can find a book on the internet and download it in one minute. It is very fast and useful.

Also, e-books are good for our eyes sometimes. You can change the size of the letters. If the letters are too small, you can make them bigger. This is very helpful for many people.

However, some people still like paper books. They like the smell of paper. They like to touch the pages with their hands. Also, paper books do not need batteries. You can read them even if there is no electricity.

In the future, both paper books and e-books will be important. We can choose the best way to read for us. Reading makes our lives better and more interesting.
