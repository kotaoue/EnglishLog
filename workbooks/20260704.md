## テーマ: The Future of Robots in Our Lives（私たちの生活の中のロボットの未来）

### 英文パッセージ

Robots are everywhere today. We can see many robots in factories and hospitals. In the future, more robots will live with us in our houses. They will help us every day.

Some robots are small and help with cleaning. They move around the floor and pick up dust. We do not need to use a big vacuum cleaner. This saves a lot of time for us. We can read books or watch TV while the robot cleans the room.

Other robots help old people. They can talk, sing, and play games together. Some robots can even bring water or medicine. They are like kind friends. Many old people feel happy and safe with them.

In schools, robots can help students and teachers. They can teach English, science, or math. Students can ask the same question many times. Robots never get tired and they are always patient.

In the future, robots will do many difficult jobs for us. They will cook delicious dinner and wash many clothes. They will also drive cars and buses safely. Because of this, we will have more free time to enjoy our hobbies.

Robots are very smart and useful, but they are different from humans. They do not have feelings or hearts like we do. We must think about how to live with them. If we use robots in a good way, our future will be very easy and exciting.
