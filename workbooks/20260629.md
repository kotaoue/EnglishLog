## テーマ: The Power of Renewable Energy（再生可能エネルギーの力）

### 英文パッセージ

Energy is very important for our lives. We use it for everything. We need energy to cook food. We need energy to use the internet. We need energy to drive cars. Most energy comes from oil, gas, and coal. We call these "fossil fuels." However, fossil fuels have two big problems. First, they will run out one day. We cannot make more oil or coal easily. Second, they produce CO2. This makes the earth too warm. This is a big problem for nature.

Now, many people are looking at "renewable energy." This energy comes from natural things. For example, solar power comes from the sun. Wind power comes from the wind. There is also power from rivers and heat from the ground. We call these "clean" because they do not make the air dirty. Also, the sun and wind never stop. We can use them forever.

In the business world, many companies are changing. They want to use only "green" energy for their offices and factories. They build big solar farms and tall wind turbines. But this is not always easy. Sometimes the sun does not shine at night. Sometimes the wind does not blow. So, we need big batteries to save the energy for later. Scientists are working hard every day to make better batteries.

The way we make energy is changing fast. It is a big challenge for us. If we use more renewable energy, we can protect our planet. It is very important for our future.

### 設問

1. 石油や石炭などの「化石燃料（fossil fuels）」には、どのような2つの大きな問題がありますか？
   - あなたの回答: 簡単に再生産ができないのでいつか枯渇する。CO2を排出して地球温暖化を進めてしまう

2. 「再生可能エネルギー（renewable energy）」の例として、本文ではどのようなものが挙げられていますか？
   - あなたの回答: 太陽電池、風力発電、地熱発電

3. なぜ再生可能エネルギーは「クリーン（clean）」だと言われているのですか？
   - あなたの回答: 大気汚染をせず、太陽や風邪など止まることのないエネルギーを利用できる

4. 本文中の "run out" はどのような意味で使われていますか？
   - あなたの回答: なくなる。資源を使い切る

5. この文章の筆者が最も伝えたい、私たちの未来にとって重要なことは何ですか？
   - あなたの回答: もっと再生可能エネルギーを使うようにすること

2. 本文の最後に登場する "protect" はどういう意味ですか？また、何を protect したいと述べていますか？
