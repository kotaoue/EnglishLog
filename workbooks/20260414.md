## 本日の問題

はい、承知いたしました。英語の先生として、入門レベルの学習者の方が「英語を読めるようになりたい」という目標を達成できるよう、中学1〜2年レベルの基本単語のみを使った英語問題を作成します。

---

## テーマ: 私の毎日の生活

### 英文パッセージ

My name is Kaito. I am a student. I live in a big city. I like my daily life.

Every morning, I get up at 7 o'clock. I eat breakfast with my family. My mother makes delicious breakfast for us. Then, I go to school by train. It is a little far from my home. School starts at 8:30. I study many subjects at school, like math and science. My favorite subject is English. I like to read English books. It helps me learn new words.

At lunchtime, I eat lunch with my friends in the cafeteria. We talk and laugh a lot. After school, I often go to the library. I read many kinds of books there. Sometimes, I play soccer with my friends in the park. We play for an hour. It is very fun to play sports. I like to move my body.

In the evening, I go home. I eat dinner with my family. We always talk about our day. After dinner, I do my homework for about one hour. Then, I watch TV or listen to music. I often listen to pop music and rock music. I go to bed at 11 o'clock every night.

On weekends, I relax at home. I sometimes go to the movie theater with my friends. We watch interesting movies and eat popcorn. I also help my mother with house chores. I clean my room. I like my family very much. My life is busy, but it is always happy. I want to learn many new things every day.

### 設問

1. Kaitoは毎朝、何時ごろに起きて、誰と朝食を食べますか？
    - あなたの回答: 7:00

2. Kaitoが学校で一番好きな科目は何ですか？また、なぜその科目が好きですか？
    - あなたの回答: 英語

3. Kaitoは放課後、友達と公園で何をすることがありますか？それは彼にとってどのような活動ですか？
    - あなたの回答: サッカー

4. 英文中の "delicious" という単語は、食べ物についてどのようなことを表す言葉ですか？
    - あなたの回答: 美味しい

5. この英文全体を通して、Kaitoは自分の生活についてどのように感じていると読み取れますか？
    - あなたの回答: 忙しいけど幸せで、毎日新しいことを学んでいる
