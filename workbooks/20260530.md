## テーマ: My Wonderful Town (私の素敵な町)

### 英文パッセージ

I live in a small town. Its name is Green Hills. Green Hills is a very nice place. It is not a big city. It is a quiet town. My town is near a beautiful river. Many green trees are along the river. I often walk there with my dog, Max. It is relaxing to walk by the river. The air is fresh.

There is a big park in Green Hills. Kids play in the park every day. They run and jump. We can see many colorful flowers in spring and summer. In autumn, the leaves turn red and yellow. People have picnics there when the weather is good. It is a happy place for everyone in my town.

There are also some important stores. There is a supermarket where I buy food for my family. There is also a small book store. I like to read, so I often visit it to find new books. My school is also in Green Hills. My friends and I go to school together every morning. We learn many new things every day from our kind teachers.

The streets in Green Hills are always clean. People are friendly here. They often say "Hello" to me on the street. I feel safe and happy in my town. In the evening, the town is very calm. I like to watch the stars from my window. Sometimes, I hear the sound of the river.

On weekends, I sometimes go to the local cafe. I drink juice and talk with my friends. It is a fun time for us. My town is not big or famous, but it is special to me. I love my wonderful town very much. It is my cozy home.
