## テーマ: The History and Future of Money (お金の歴史と未来)

### 英文パッセージ

Long ago, people did not use money. They traded things like fish, salt, or fruit. This was the first way to get things. However, it was difficult to trade many things. For example, fish do not stay fresh for a long time. Later, people began to use coins. They made coins from gold or silver. These coins were heavy but very strong. Then, people used paper money. Paper money was light and easy to carry in a bag.

Today, our world is changing again. Many people use credit cards and smartphones to pay. We call this a "cashless society." You do not need to carry coins or bills in your pocket. You just touch your phone to a small machine in a shop. This is very fast and easy. Also, many people buy clothes and books on the internet. They use digital money to pay for them.

However, there are some problems. Some people worry about digital money. Sometimes computers or machines do not work. Then, we cannot buy anything. Also, bad people like hackers can steal digital money. We must protect our personal information. In the future, physical money may disappear from our lives. We will use only digital data for shopping. Is this a good thing or a bad thing? We need to think about it. Money is changing every day. We must learn how to use it safely and wisely.

### 設問

1. 昔の人々は、お金の代わりにどのようにして欲しいものを手に入れていましたか？
   - あなたの回答: 魚や塩やフルーツ等を物々交換していた

2. 硬貨（coins）と紙幣（paper money）のそれぞれの特徴について、本文ではどのように説明されていますか？
   - あなたの回答: 硬貨は重いが丈夫。紙は軽くて持ち運びしやすい

3. 「キャッシュレス社会」において、買い物をする時はどのような動作をしますか？
   - あなたの回答: スマホを機械にタッチする

4. 本文の中で、デジタルマネーの欠点として挙げられていることを2つ答えてください。
   - あなたの回答: コンピューターや機械が動作しない状態だと無力。わるい人がハックして盗んだりする

5. この文章の筆者は、将来のお金について読者にどのようなメッセージを伝えていますか？
   - あなたの回答: 物理的なお金を使う機会は減り、電子マネーがメインになるが、それが良いか悪いかを考える必要がある
