## 本日の問題

## テーマ: 私の一日

### 英文パッセージ

Hi, I am Ken. I am a student. I live in Japan. My day starts early. I wake up at seven o'clock. I eat breakfast with my family. My mother makes good food. I like eggs and milk.

After breakfast, I go to school. I walk to school. My school is not far. It takes about fifteen minutes. My school has many rooms. I study many subjects there. My favorite subject is English. I like to read English books. My English teacher is kind. She helps us a lot.

At noon, I eat lunch. I eat lunch with my friends. We talk and laugh. After school, I go home. Sometimes, I play soccer in the park with my friends. We have fun. Then I do my homework. It is important.

In the evening, I eat dinner with my family. We talk about our day. After dinner, I read a book or watch TV. I like to relax. I go to bed at ten o'clock. I sleep well. This is my day. It is a good day.

### 設問

1. ケンは何時に起きますか？
    - あなたの回答: 7時

2. ケンは学校で、どの科目を勉強するのが一番好きですか？
    - あなたの回答: 英語

3. 放課後、ケンは時々公園で何をしますか？
    - あなたの回答: サッカー

4. 英文中の "kind" という単語はどのような意味ですか？
    - あなたの回答: 親切

5. この英文は主に何について書かれていますか？
    - あなたの回答: ケンの1日
