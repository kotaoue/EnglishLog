## テーマ: The World of Robots (ロボットの世界)

### 英文パッセージ

Robots are everywhere today. You can see them in houses, schools, and offices. Many people like robots because they help us a lot.

First, let's look at robots at home. Many families use small robots to clean their rooms. These robots move on the floor and take away dust. They are very smart. Some robots can also help in the kitchen. They can make coffee or help with cooking. This saves time for busy people.

Second, robots work in big buildings. In some hospitals, robots carry medicine to patients. They know the way to every room. They do not get lost. In some shops, robots say "Hello" to customers. They can tell you where the milk is or where the bread is.

Third, robots are good for dangerous work. They go to places where people cannot go. For example, they can go deep into the ocean or into hot places. They take photos and give us information.

However, robots and humans are different. Robots are good at doing the same work many times. They do not get tired or bored. But robots cannot feel happy or sad. They do not have hearts. Humans can think and feel.

In the future, we will live with robots every day. They will be our friends and partners. We must learn how to use them well. What kind of robot do you want? Let's imagine a fun future with robots.
