## テーマ: My Daily Routine (私の毎日の日課)

### 英文パッセージ

My name is Ken. I am a student. I live in a big city.
Every morning, I wake up at 7 o'clock. I wash my face and brush my teeth.
Then, I eat breakfast. I like to eat bread and drink milk.
After breakfast, I go to school. My school is not far from my house. I walk to school with my friends.
At school, I study many subjects. I like English and Math. My English teacher is Mr. Tanaka. He is a good teacher.
We have lunch at school. I eat my lunch box. My mother makes it. It is always yummy.
In the afternoon, I play soccer with my friends. We play in the schoolyard. It is fun.
After school, I go home. I do my homework in my room. It is sometimes difficult, but I try my best.
Then, I help my mother. I clean my room or wash the dishes.
In the evening, my family eats dinner together. We talk about our day.
After dinner, I read a book or watch TV. I like animal shows.
At 10 o'clock, I go to bed. I am often tired, but I am happy every day.
This is my daily life. It is a simple life, but I enjoy it very much.
