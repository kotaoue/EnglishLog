## テーマ: Healthy Daily Habits (健康的な毎日の習慣)

### 英文パッセージ

Hello. My name is Ken. I want to talk about a healthy life. A healthy life is very important for everyone. It makes us happy and strong. We all want to feel good every day. So, let's learn about good habits.

First, let's talk about food. We need to eat good food every day. Eat many vegetables and fruits. For example, apples, oranges, and carrots are good. They have many vitamins. Vitamins are good for our body. Drink a lot of water, too. Water helps our body work well. Try to drink 8 glasses of water a day. Do not eat too much candy or fast food. They are not good for your teeth and body in the long run.

Next, let's talk about exercise. We need to move our body. Walking is a good exercise. You can walk to school or to the park. It is easy and fun. Playing sports is also good. You can play soccer or tennis with your friends. Exercise makes your body strong and gives you energy. Try to exercise for 30 minutes every day.

After exercise, rest is important. We need to sleep well. Sleep 7 or 8 hours every night. Going to bed early is good. Good sleep makes your mind clear. You can study better and feel less tired after good sleep.

Also, spending time with family and friends is good for your mind. Talk with them. Laugh with them. This makes your mind happy. A happy mind helps your body stay healthy. It reduces stress.

So, remember these simple things. Eat good food. Exercise every day. Sleep well. And spend time with loved ones. These simple steps help you live a healthy and happy life. Let's try to follow them together!

### 設問

1. 筆者は、健康的な生活のために、どんな食べ物や飲み物が大切だと言っていますか？具体的な例を２つ挙げてください。
    - あなたの回答: 野菜や果物、たくさんの水

2. 体を強くし、私たちにエネルギーを与えてくれるものは何ですか？
    - あなたの回答: 運動

3. 家族や友人と過ごす時間は、私たちの心にどんな良い影響を与えますか？
    - あなたの回答: 幸せになる

4. 英文中の「Water helps our body **work well**.」の "work well" は、この文脈でどのような意味になりますか？最も近いものを選んでください。
    (A. うまく働く B. 疲れる C. 休む)
    - あなたの回答: A

5. この英文の主な目的は何ですか？
    - あなたの回答: 健康のための習慣
