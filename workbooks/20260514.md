## テーマ: Sustainable Living for Everyone (みんなのための持続可能な生活)

### 英文パッセージ

Living a sustainable life might sound difficult, but it's actually about making small, eco-friendly choices every day. These choices, when combined, can have a big positive impact on our planet and future generations.

One important step is to be mindful of what you consume. For instance, fast fashion contributes significantly to waste and resource depletion. Opting for durable, high-quality clothes that last longer, or even exploring second-hand stores, can make a difference. When it comes to food, choosing local and seasonal products not only supports local farmers but also reduces carbon emissions associated with transportation. Bringing your own reusable bags to the grocery store is another simple, effective habit.

Next, consider your energy consumption. Simple actions like turning off lights when leaving a room, unplugging electronic devices when not in use, and adjusting your thermostat can save a lot of electricity. Furthermore, reducing reliance on private cars by using public transport, cycling, or walking helps decrease air pollution and promotes a healthier lifestyle. If you have a garden, collecting rainwater can be an excellent way to conserve water.

Finally, remember the "3 Rs": Reduce, Reuse, and Recycle. Reduce means minimizing your purchases and avoiding products with excessive packaging. Reuse encourages finding new purposes for old items, such as transforming glass jars into storage containers. Recycle involves properly sorting your waste so that materials like plastic, paper, and glass can be processed and reborn as new products.

While some people believe sustainable living is expensive or inconvenient, many eco-friendly practices can actually save money over time and lead to a more fulfilling life. Every small action, consistently practiced, counts towards protecting our beautiful Earth. By making conscious choices, we can all contribute to a healthier, more sustainable world for everyone.
