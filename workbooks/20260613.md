## テーマ: Recycling for Our Earth

### 英文パッセージ

Recycling is important for our planet. We can save our Earth by recycling. It means we use old things again to make new things. This helps to reduce trash. Less trash is good for nature.

Many things can be recycled. For example, we can recycle paper, plastic bottles, glass bottles, and metal cans. When we finish a drink, we can put the plastic bottle in a special bin. When we read a newspaper, we can put the old paper in another bin. This is called separating trash.

It is easy to recycle. First, we separate our trash at home. We put paper with paper, plastic with plastic, and so on. Then, we take them to a recycling center or put them out for collection. Many towns have a system for this.

Recycling also saves energy. Making new things from recycled materials uses less energy than making them from new materials. This helps to protect our planet. When we recycle, we help animals and plants too. We make our world a cleaner place.

Every small act of recycling makes a big difference. Let's recycle together. Let's make our Earth a beautiful place for everyone.
