## テーマ: Healthy Daily Habits (健康的な毎日の習慣)

### 英文パッセージ

Many people want to live a healthy life. It is very important for our body and our mind.

First, we should eat good food every day. Fruits and vegetables are very healthy. They give us vitamins and energy. We should try not to eat too much sugar or fast food. These foods can be bad for our body if we eat them often.

Second, exercise is also important. You can walk, run, or play sports. Doing some exercise for about 30 minutes each day is good. It makes our body strong. It also makes our heart strong.

Third, sleep is necessary. We need to get enough sleep every night. For most people, 7 or 8 hours of sleep is good. When we sleep, our body rests and repairs itself. It helps us think clearly the next day.

Fourth, drink enough water. Water is very important for our body. It helps all parts of our body work well. Always carry a water bottle with you.

Fifth, try to relax and manage stress. Stress can be bad for our health. You can read a book, listen to music, or talk with friends. Sometimes, go outside and feel the sun. Fresh air is good for you.

These simple habits can help us feel good. They can make us healthy and happy every day. Let's start today!
