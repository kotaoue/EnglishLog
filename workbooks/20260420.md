## テーマ: Healthy Daily Life (健康的な毎日)

### 英文パッセージ

Hi. My name is Anna. I want to tell you about my daily life. I wake up at 6 o'clock every morning. I drink a glass of water. It is good for my body. This helps me feel fresh.

Then I eat breakfast. I usually eat fruit and bread. My favorite fruit is an apple. I also drink milk. Breakfast gives me energy for the day. It is a very important meal.

After breakfast, I walk to school. I like to walk. It is good exercise. My school is not far from my house. I see many trees and flowers on my way.

In the afternoon, I study at school. I learn many things. My favorite subject is English. I like to read English books. I also practice speaking English with my friends.

After school, I sometimes play with my friends. We play in the park. We run and laugh together. It is fun. Sometimes we just talk.

In the evening, I eat dinner with my family. My mom cooks delicious food. We talk about our day. We like to share our stories.

Before I sleep, I read a book. I like to read many stories. Then I go to bed at 9 o'clock. I sleep for 8 hours. Good sleep is very important for a healthy life.

These are my healthy habits. They make me happy and strong every day. I try to follow them always.
