## テーマ: Healthy Daily Habits (健康的な毎日の習慣)

### 英文パッセージ

Living a healthy life is very important for everyone. It helps us feel good every day. Let's think about some simple habits.

First, good food is key. We should eat many fruits and vegetables. Apples, oranges, and bananas are good examples. Vegetables like carrots and tomatoes are also healthy. These foods give our bodies energy. They help us grow strong. We also need to drink a lot of water. Water helps our body work well. Try to drink water often, not just when you feel thirsty.

Next, exercise is important for our body. We do not need to do hard sports every day. Simple activities are also good. For example, walking is a great exercise. You can walk to school or walk with your family in a park. Playing outside with friends is also a fun way to move your body. Exercise makes our muscles strong and our hearts healthy. It can also make us feel happy.

Finally, getting enough sleep is very important. Our bodies need to rest after a busy day. Children need about 9 to 11 hours of sleep every night. Adults need about 7 to 9 hours. When we sleep well, we can think better and feel fresh in the morning. We can also learn new things easily.

So, remember these three habits: eat good food and drink water, exercise often, and get enough sleep. These small things can make a big difference in your healthy life. Start today!
