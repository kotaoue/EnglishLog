## テーマ: 私の新しい趣味：ガーデニング

### 英文パッセージ

Last month, I started a new hobby. It is gardening. I like plants and flowers very much. My small garden is in my backyard. It is a nice place.

First, I went to a store and bought some seeds. They were very small. I also bought some new tools, like a small shovel. Then, I put the seeds in the soft soil. I always remember to put water on them every morning.

After about two weeks, I saw something green. Small green leaves appeared from the soil. I was very happy and excited. Day by day, my plants are getting bigger and taller. They need a lot of sunlight to grow. So, I often move their pots to a sunny place in the garden.

Gardening is sometimes a little difficult, but it is always fun. I am learning many new things about plants and nature. Sometimes, my mother helps me. She knows a lot about flowers. I often talk to my plants. They are like my quiet friends.

Soon, I hope to see beautiful, colorful flowers. Or I want to pick fresh, tasty vegetables from my garden. My garden is a good place for me to relax. I spend time there after school and on weekends. It makes me feel good and happy. I want to grow many more kinds of plants next year.
