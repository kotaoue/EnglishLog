## テーマ: AIと私たちの日常生活

### 英文パッセージ

Artificial Intelligence, or AI, is no longer just a concept from science fiction. It has become an integral part of our daily lives, often without us even realizing it. From the moment we wake up until we go to sleep, AI is working behind the scenes.

Think about your smartphone. AI powers many features, such as facial recognition to unlock your device, voice assistants like Siri or Google Assistant, and personalized recommendations on social media or shopping apps. When you stream music or movies, AI suggests what you might like next based on your viewing history. Even the grammar checker in your writing software uses AI to help you improve your sentences.

One of the biggest advantages of AI is its ability to process vast amounts of data quickly and efficiently. This can save us time and make tasks easier. For example, AI-powered translation apps can help us understand different languages instantly, making travel and communication much smoother. In the future, AI is expected to play an even larger role, from self-driving cars to advanced medical diagnoses.

However, it's also important to be aware of the challenges. Concerns about privacy, data security, and the potential impact on jobs are often discussed. As AI becomes more powerful, we need to learn how to use it wisely and responsibly. Understanding how AI works and thinking critically about the information it provides will be key to navigating this technological future. Embracing AI while remaining thoughtful will help us make the most of its potential.
