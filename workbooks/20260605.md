## テーマ: The Joy of Cooking (料理の喜び)

### 英文パッセージ

Cooking is fun. Many people like to cook. It is a good activity. You can make yummy food. You can cook for your family. It is good for your body. You know what you eat. Let's talk about cooking.

First, choose a simple recipe. For example, you can make a salad. You need some vegetables. You need tomatoes, lettuce, and cucumber. Wash them well. Cut them into small pieces. Put them in a big bowl. Add some olive oil and salt. Mix them gently. Your fresh salad is ready.

You can also make a simple sandwich. Take two slices of bread. Put some cheese and ham. Add some lettuce. It is a quick meal. Maybe you like sweet food. You can try to make cookies. You need flour, sugar, and butter. Mix them well. Put them in the oven. Wait for some minutes. The smell is wonderful.

Cooking can be a family activity. Parents and children can cook together. They can talk and laugh. It makes a special time. Cooking teaches you many things. You learn to follow steps. You learn to be careful. You learn about different foods.

It helps you save money too. Eating out costs more. When you cook at home, you save money. It is a healthy choice. Fresh ingredients are good for you. Cooking can be a good hobby. You can learn new recipes. You can try foods from other countries. It is like an adventure. When you cook, you use your hands. You use your eyes. You use your nose. It is good for your brain.

Share your food with friends. They will be happy. Cooking connects people. It is more than just making food. It is about love. It is about health. It is about happiness. So, try cooking today. Start with something easy. You will enjoy it. Your family will enjoy it too. Cooking brings joy to your home. It is a wonderful skill.
