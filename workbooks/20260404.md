## テーマ: 私の一日 (My Day)

### 英文パッセージ

Hello! My name is Ken. I am a student. I wake up at 7 o'clock every morning. First, I eat breakfast. I like toast and milk. Then, I go to school. My school is not far from my home. I walk to school with my friends. We talk and laugh on the way.

School starts at 8:30. I study many subjects. I have English, Math, Science, and Art classes. My favorite subject is English. I like to learn new words. Our English teacher is Mr. Tanaka. He is very kind and funny. He helps us understand difficult sentences.

At noon, we eat lunch. I usually eat a sandwich or rice. After lunch, we play outside for a short time. I play soccer with my friends. It is fun!

School finishes at 3:00 in the afternoon. I go home. I do my homework. Sometimes, I read a book. I like stories about animals. In the evening, I eat dinner with my family. We talk about our day. Before bed, I watch TV for a little while. I go to sleep at 9 o'clock. This is my day.

### 設問

1. ケンは毎朝何時に起きますか？
    - あなたの回答: 7時

2. ケンの好きな科目は何ですか？
    - あなたの回答: トーストとミルク

3. ケンは放課後（学校が終わった後）、何をしますか？
    - あなたの回答: 宿題と読書と家族と夕食

4. 英文中の "kind" という単語は、どのような意味ですか？
    - あなたの回答: 親切

5. この英文パッセージは主に何について書かれていますか？
    - あなたの回答: 学校のある日のケンの1日
