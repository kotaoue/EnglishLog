## テーマ: Smartphones: Our Daily Friends

### 英文パッセージ

Smartphones are very common today. Many people have one. We use smartphones for many things. They are very useful tools.

First, we use them to talk to people. We can call our family and friends. We can also send messages. This is a quick way to communicate.

Second, smartphones give us a lot of information. We can search for news on the internet. We can also check the weather or find a good restaurant.

Third, smartphones are fun. We can listen to music or watch videos. Many people play games on their phones. They are also good for learning. We can use apps to study English or history. Some people use them for work or school.

Smartphones help us find places with maps. We never get lost easily. We can take beautiful photos and videos with them. They are small and easy to carry in our bags or pockets.

So, smartphones are like small computers in our hands. They connect us to the world. They make our lives easier and more convenient. But we should use them carefully. Too much time on the phone is not good for our eyes. We need to find a good balance. Smartphones are a big part of our daily lives. They are our good friends.
