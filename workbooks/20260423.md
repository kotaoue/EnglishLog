## テーマ: 健康的な生活を送ろう (Let's Live a Healthy Life)

### 英文パッセージ

A healthy life is very important for us. It makes us happy. It helps us feel good every day.

First, we should eat good food. We need to eat many vegetables and fruits. They have vitamins. Vitamins are good for our body. We also need to drink much water. Water helps our body work well. We should not eat too much fast food. Fast food is not very healthy.

Second, we should move our body. Exercise is important. We can walk every day. We can play sports with friends. Walking is a simple exercise. Sports make us strong. It is good to exercise for 30 minutes.

Third, we need to sleep enough. Good sleep gives us energy. We should sleep for about 7 or 8 hours. When we sleep well, we can study and work better.

Also, it is important to relax. We can read books. We can listen to music. We can talk with our family or friends. These things help us feel calm.

A healthy life is a happy life. Let's try to live healthy every day. It is a good goal for everyone.
