   - **採点: 正解**
   - **解説:** 英文の該当箇所「Fruits are excellent. Apples, bananas, and oranges have many vitamins.」「Eggs are also a great choice. They have protein.」「Milk or yogurt is good for strong bones. They have calcium, a very important mineral.」を正確にまとめています。栄養素にも触れられていて素晴らしいです。
