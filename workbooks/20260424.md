## テーマ: A Balanced Life in the Digital Age (デジタル時代のバランスの取れた生活)

### 英文パッセージ

In today's fast-paced world, technology has become an **inseparable** part of our daily lives. From smartphones to laptops, we use various devices for communication, work, entertainment, and learning. This has brought immense **convenience** and has greatly increased our **productivity**. For example, we can connect with people across the globe instantly, and access vast amounts of information with just a few clicks.

However, this constant connection also presents challenges to our well-being. Many people spend an **excessive** amount of time in front of screens, often leading to a **sedentary** lifestyle. This lack of physical activity can contribute to various health issues, including eye strain, sleep problems, and even mental stress. It’s important to remember that our physical and mental health are just as important as our digital connectivity.

To maintain a healthy balance, it is suggested that we practice "digital detox." This means consciously taking breaks from our devices and engaging in other activities. Spending time outdoors, exercising, reading physical books, or simply having face-to-face conversations can be very beneficial. These activities can help us reconnect with nature, improve our physical health, and reduce stress. Mindfulness practices, like meditation, can also help us stay present and focused.

In conclusion, technology offers wonderful opportunities, but we must use it wisely. By being **mindful** of our screen time and actively seeking alternative activities, we can enjoy the benefits of the digital age without sacrificing our overall health and happiness. A balanced approach is key to a fulfilling life in this modern era.
