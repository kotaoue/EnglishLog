## テーマ: The Secrets of Sleep (睡眠の秘密)

### 英文パッセージ

Sleep is very important for everyone. We spend about one-third of our lives sleeping. But why do we sleep? Some people think sleep is just resting. However, sleep is more than that. Scientists say sleep helps our brain and body in many ways.

When we sleep, our body becomes very quiet. But our brain is still working hard. The brain cleans itself during the night. It also fixes and saves the things we learned during the day. For example, if you study English today and sleep well, your brain saves that information. You can remember it better the next day. This is why students should sleep enough before a test.

Sleep is also good for our health. It helps our heart and our body parts. If we do not sleep enough, we feel tired and sleepy the next day. We may feel sad or get angry easily. We cannot work well at our jobs, and we cannot enjoy our hobbies. 

How many hours do we need to sleep? It is different for everyone. Most adults need seven or eight hours of sleep. Children and young people need more. They need about nine or ten hours because their bodies are growing. 

Many people today use smartphones or computers before they sleep. But this is not good for our sleep. The blue light from these screens is very strong. It tells the brain, "The sun is up! It is morning!" Then, the brain stays awake and we cannot fall asleep. We should put away our phones one hour before we go to bed. 

Let's try to sleep well every night. It is the best way to be healthy, smart, and happy!
