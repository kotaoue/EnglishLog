## テーマ: The Joy of Virtual Travel (バーチャル旅行の楽しさ)

### 英文パッセージ

Do you like traveling? Many people love to see new places. But traveling is sometimes difficult. It is often expensive. It also takes a lot of time. Now, we have a new way to travel. It is "Virtual Reality," or VR.

With VR, you can stay at home. You wear special glasses on your head. Then, you can see any place in the world. You can visit Paris in one minute. You can see the Eiffel Tower. You can even go to the moon or under the sea. It is very exciting.

VR is very easy to use. You do not need a plane ticket. You do not need to pack a big suitcase. It is very cheap, too. Many people use VR to learn about history. They can visit old cities from long ago. They can see how people lived in the past.

Students use VR in their classrooms. They can go to the ocean and see many fish. They can go to a forest and see tall trees. It is a great way to study science. They can see things they cannot see in real life.

But VR is not the same as real travel. You cannot smell the food in the market. You cannot feel the wind on your face. You cannot talk to local people face to face.

Is VR the future of travel? Maybe it is. Many people think it is a great tool. It helps us see the world from our living room. It is a big change for our lives. We can enjoy many things with VR.
