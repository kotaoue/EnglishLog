## テーマ: Healthy Daily Habits (健康的な毎日の習慣)

### 英文パッセージ

Good morning! Let's talk about some good habits for your day. These habits can make you feel better.

First, try to wake up early. When you wake up early, you have more time. You can start your day slowly and calmly. It is good for your body and your mind. You can think clearly.

Then, eat a good breakfast every morning. Breakfast is the most important meal of the day. It gives you energy for school or work. It helps your brain work well. Do not skip breakfast!

Next, drink a lot of water. Your body needs water. Water helps your body work correctly. It keeps you hydrated. Drink water often during the day, not just when you are thirsty. Water is very important for your health.

Also, try to do some exercise. You do not need to run a marathon. Even a short walk outside is good. You can stretch your body. Exercise makes your body strong and happy. It helps you sleep better at night.

After school or work, take some time to relax. You can read a book, listen to music, or talk with your family. These activities help your mind rest. Do not always look at a screen.

Before you go to bed, try to put away your smartphone or computer. The light from screens can make it hard to sleep. It is good for your eyes and brain to rest from screens.

Finally, get enough sleep every night. Sleep is very important. Your body and mind need to rest. Most people need about 7 or 8 hours of sleep. When you sleep well, you can feel happy and strong the next day.

These simple habits can make your life much better. Try to do them every day. You can have a happy and healthy life!
