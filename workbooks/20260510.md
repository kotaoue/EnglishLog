I liked the shop because it was quiet enough to talk. We talked for a long time. It is a good place for friends to meet. You can also work or study there. They have free Wi-Fi. Many students were using their laptops.

I want to go there again soon. Maybe next weekend? Let's go together. I think you will like it. It's a very comfortable place. It opens at 8 AM every day. They close at 7 PM. I hope you can join me. We can enjoy coffee and talk.
