## テーマ: My Day at School (学校での一日)

### 英文パッセージ

My name is Kaito. I am a student. I go to school every day. My school is big and clean.

I get up at 7 o'clock in the morning. My alarm clock rings loudly. I eat breakfast with my family. My mom makes good food, like toast and eggs. I drink milk.

Then, I go to school by bus. The bus stop is near my house. I meet my friends, Ken and Sakura, there. We talk and laugh on the bus.

Our first class is English. I like English very much. Our teacher is kind and funny. She always helps us.

After that, we have math and science classes. Math is a little difficult for me, but science is fun because we do experiments.

At 12 o'clock, we eat lunch in the classroom. I eat my lunch box. My friends and I share snacks and talk a lot about many things.

In the afternoon, we have music or art. These classes are very fun. I like to draw pictures in art class.

After school, I sometimes play soccer with my friends on the school ground. Soccer is my favorite sport. Sometimes, I go home straight and help my mom.

At home, I do my homework in my room. My dad sometimes helps me with math problems.

In the evening, I eat dinner with my family. We talk about our day. Then, I read a book before bed. I like adventure books.

I go to bed at 10 o'clock. I am always a little tired but very happy. My school life is busy but good.
