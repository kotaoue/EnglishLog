## テーマ: My Favorite Hobby: Reading Books

### 英文パッセージ

My name is Ken. I am a student. I like many things, but my favorite hobby is reading books. Reading is very fun for me.

I read many kinds of books. I read story books about interesting people and places. I also read science books. They teach me about animals and the sky. Sometimes, I read history books to learn about the past.

I often go to the library. The library is a big building with many books. I can borrow books for free there. It is a good place. Sometimes, I buy new books. I go to a bookstore in my town. Bookstores have nice, new books.

When I read, I can learn many new things. Books teach me about the world. They also teach me about different people and ideas. Reading helps me to understand the world better.

Reading makes me happy. It is a quiet time for me. I often read in my room. I sit on my chair and open a book. Sometimes, I read in a park on a sunny day. I like to read under a big tree.

My friends also like books. We often talk about the books we read. We share good stories and ideas.

I think reading is a very important hobby. It helps me to think and grow. Everyone can try reading. It is a good activity for your mind. Books are wonderful friends.
