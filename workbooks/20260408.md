## テーマ: My Daily Life (私の毎日)

### 英文パッセージ

My name is Kaito. I am a student. I am 13 years old. I live with my family. We live in a big house. I have a sister. Her name is Hana. She is 10 years old. We love our family.

Every morning, I wake up at 7 o'clock. I eat breakfast with my family. My mother makes good food. I like eggs and bread. After breakfast, I go to school. My school is near my house. I walk to school every day.

At school, I study many subjects. I like English and Math. My English teacher is kind. My friends are also at school. We talk and laugh together. School is fun.

In the afternoon, I come home. I sometimes play soccer with my friends. We play in the park. It is a good time. Then, I do my homework. Homework is sometimes difficult, but I try my best.

In the evening, I eat dinner with my family. We talk about our day. After dinner, I read a book or watch TV. I like adventure books. Before I sleep, I brush my teeth. I go to bed at 9 o'clock.

On weekends, I am free. I often go to the library. I read many books there. Sometimes, I go to the cinema with my sister. We like funny movies. My family also goes to the park. We enjoy walking there. I love my daily life. It is busy but happy.
