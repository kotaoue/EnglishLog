## テーマ: 私たちの生活とテクノロジー

### 英文パッセージ

Today, many people use smart phones. These phones are very useful tools. We can talk to friends and family with them. We can also send messages quickly. Our phones help us in many ways every day.

For example, we can find information on the internet. If you want to know about a new place, you can search for it easily. You can see pictures and maps. This makes traveling and planning trips very easy for everyone.

Also, smart phones have many apps. Some apps help us learn new languages like English. Some apps help us play fun games. Other apps help us manage our money or track our health. They make our lives more convenient.

Computers are also very important in our lives. We use computers for work and for study. Students use them to learn new things at school. Teachers use them to prepare lessons and teach students.

The internet connects us all. It helps us share ideas with people far away. It helps us read news from other countries around the world. The internet is a big and important part of our daily life now.

These technologies like phones and computers make our lives better in many ways. They can save our time. They give us new experiences and knowledge. But it is important to use them wisely. We should also remember to enjoy time without screens and talk to people face-to-face.

Technology is always changing and growing. New things come out every year. We should learn how to use these new tools well. It helps us live a good and happy life in the modern world.
