## テーマ: The World of Virtual Reality（仮想現実の世界）

### 英文パッセージ

Virtual Reality (VR) is a new technology. We use a headset on our eyes. We see a computer world. It looks very real. We can look around. We see the sky above. We see the ground below.

Many people like VR games. In a game, you can be a hero. You can fight monsters. You can also play sports with friends. They are far away, but you feel close.

VR is good for work and study too. For example, doctors use it. They practice difficult work with VR. It is safe for sick people. Pilots also use VR. They practice flying in bad weather. They do not use a real plane, so it is safe.

Schools use VR. Students can visit the forest. They can see dinosaurs. They can go back to old times. This is very interesting for students. They like learning with VR.

Companies use VR for meetings. You are at home, but your friend is in the office. You put on the headset. Now, you are in the same room. You can talk and see each other.

In the future, VR will change more. Now, headsets are big and heavy. Future headsets will be like glasses. They will be very light. We can use them every day. We will feel things and smell things in VR.

VR opens a new door. We can go anywhere and be anyone. The world of VR is waiting for you.
