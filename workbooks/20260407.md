## テーマ: My Favorite Season: Spring

### 英文パッセージ

My name is Aki. I like spring very much.
Spring is a beautiful season. The weather is warm. It is not cold or hot.
The sun shines brightly in the sky. Many flowers open their petals.
I like to see colorful flowers. They are very pretty. Red, yellow, and blue flowers are everywhere.
Birds sing happy songs. I can hear them every morning. Their songs are sweet.
People go outside. They walk in parks. They enjoy the fresh air.
Children play with friends. They laugh and run. They have much fun under the sun.
I often go to the park with my family.
My dog, Pochi, also likes spring. He runs fast in the park. He plays with a ball.
We like to sit on a bench. We eat lunch there together.
Sometimes, we ride bikes. My father, my mother, and I ride bikes. It is good exercise for us.
In spring, everything is new. Trees have green leaves. The world looks green and fresh.
Animals wake up from their long sleep. They start new lives.
I feel happy in spring. I like the fresh air. It smells so good.
It is a good time for new beginnings. Many things start in spring.
School starts in spring too. I meet new teachers and new friends.
I love spring. It is my favorite season of the year.
