## テーマ: The Magic of Smartwatches（スマートウォッチの魔法）

### 英文パッセージ

Many people have a watch today. Some watches are very special. They are called smartwatches. A smartwatch is like a small computer. You wear it on your arm like a normal watch. It is very light and useful.

A smartwatch can do many things for you. It can tell the time. It can show your new messages. You can see your emails on the small screen. You do not need to look at your smartphone all the time.

Smartwatches are very good for your health. They count your steps every day. Do you walk a lot? The watch tells you the answer. Walking is good for your heart and body. Many people want to walk 10,000 steps every day.

The watch can check your heart too. It knows your heart beat. It also checks your sleep. You sleep every night. The watch tells you about your sleep. Is your sleep good? Or is it bad? The watch shows the data on your smartphone.

Some watches talk to you. They say, "It is time to stand up!" or "Please walk now!" This is good for people in offices. They sit for a long time. The watch helps them move.

Doctors like smartwatches too. Patients show their health data to doctors. It helps the doctors understand the body. This is a new way to stay healthy.

Smartwatches will change our lives more. They are not just for time. They are for our health and our future. We will use them every day.
