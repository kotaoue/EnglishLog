## テーマ: The Future of Transportation (未来の乗り物)

### 英文パッセージ

People move from one place to another every day. Long ago, people walked or used horses. Then, trains and cars changed our lives. Today, the way we travel is changing again. This is very exciting for everyone.

Many people use cars with gasoline now. However, gasoline cars produce CO2. This is bad for our planet. So, more people are choosing electric cars. Electric cars are very quiet. They do not use gasoline, so they are clean. Many countries want to use only electric cars in the future.

Have you heard about self-driving cars? These cars use computers and cameras to see the road. They can drive by themselves. You do not need to use your hands or feet. In the future, you can work or watch movies inside your car. This technology will make driving safer. Most car accidents happen because of human mistakes.

Flying cars are also a big dream. They are like large drones with seats. They can fly over traffic jams. If we use flying cars, we can reach places very quickly. Some companies are testing these cars now. They might be in the sky soon.

In big cities, electric bikes and small scooters are popular. They are small and easy to use. People use them to go to the office or a station. They are good for short trips.

Transportation in the future will be more convenient. We can use our time better. We can also protect our environment. The world will become smaller and cleaner.
