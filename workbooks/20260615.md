## テーマ: Music in Our Lives

### 英文パッセージ

Music is very important in our lives. We listen to music almost every day. It makes us feel many emotions. We can feel happy when we hear a fun song. We can feel calm when we listen to soft music. It can also make us feel excited for something.

There are many kinds of music. Some people like pop music from today. Others like classical music from a long time ago. Rock music is also very popular with many young people. You can choose any kind of music you like. There is music for everyone.

Music can help us in different ways. When we study, quiet music can help us focus better. When we do sports, fast music can give us more energy to run or jump. It can also help us relax after a long day of work or school. Many people listen to music before they sleep to feel calm and get good rest.

People around the world enjoy music. They sing songs together. They play various musical instruments like a guitar or a piano. We can go to concerts to hear live music. Music connects people from different places. Even if we speak different languages, we can share the same feelings through music. It is truly a universal language that everyone understands.

Music is a wonderful part of our culture and our daily life. It brings joy to many people. So, let's listen to more music. It is good for our hearts and our minds.
