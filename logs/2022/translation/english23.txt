Men Hugging Other Men

An interesting cultural trend has emergend in the U.S. in recent years.
More and more men are greeting friends, including male friends, by hugging them.
It seems that aget plays somewhat of a role, as younger men are more likely to hug.
For many older men, hugging is outside of their comfort zone.

Even among men who aer open to hugging, many belive is's generally taboo in the workplace.
However, in relaxed social gatherings, particulaky among friends, it's appropriate for them.
They wouldn't necessarily hug another guy when meeting for the first time, but after establishing a friendship, huggin is just fine.

Huggin is also becoming popular among politicians.
When President Trump welcomed Prime Minister Abe recently, he hugged him and spoke of the "very good chemistry" between the two of them.
